Football, also known as soccer in some countries, is played between two teams of eleven players each.
A standard football match consists of two 45-minute halves, for a total of 90 minutes of play.
The objective of football is to score more goals than the opposing team by getting the ball into the goal.
Only the goalkeeper is permitted to use their hands and arms within their own penalty area.
The FIFA World Cup is held every four years and is the most prestigious tournament in international football.
The UEFA Champions League is the top annual club competition among Europe's elite football teams.
A red card results in a player being sent off and their team playing with one fewer player for the rest of the match.
A yellow card serves as an official caution to a player for unsporting behavior or a rule violation.
An offside violation occurs when an attacking player is nearer to the opponent's goal line than both the ball and the second-last defender when the ball is played to them.
Pele won three FIFA World Cups with Brazil and is widely regarded as one of the greatest players in football history.
Diego Maradona led Argentina to World Cup glory in 1986 and is celebrated for his individual skill.
Lionel Messi and Cristiano Ronaldo are among the most decorated goal scorers in modern football history.
A hat-trick in football refers to a single player scoring three goals in one match.
The football pitch typically measures between 100 and 130 yards in length and 50 to 100 yards in width.
A penalty kick is awarded when a foul is committed by the defending team inside their own penalty area.
The Premier League is one of the most widely watched domestic football leagues in the world, based in England.
Extra time and penalty shootouts are used to determine a winner in knockout matches that end level after regulation.
FIFA, the Federation Internationale de Football Association, is the sport's global governing body.
The first official FIFA World Cup was held in Uruguay in 1930.
A corner kick is awarded to the attacking team when the ball goes out of play over the goal line after last touching a defender.
