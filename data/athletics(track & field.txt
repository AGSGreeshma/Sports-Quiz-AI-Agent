Athletics, also known as Track and Field, is a collection of sporting events involving running, jumping, throwing, and walking.

World Athletics is the international governing body.

Athletics is one of the oldest Olympic sports and forms the centerpiece of the Summer Olympic Games.

Running events include:
100m
200m
400m
800m
1500m
5000m
10000m
Marathon
Hurdles
Relay races

Field events include:
Long Jump
Triple Jump
High Jump
Pole Vault
Shot Put
Discus Throw
Hammer Throw
Javelin Throw

Combined events include the Decathlon and Heptathlon.

Usain Bolt holds the world records in the men's 100m and 200m sprints.

Major championships include the Olympic Games, World Athletics Championships, and Diamond League.

Athletes compete to achieve the fastest time, greatest distance, or greatest height depending on the event.
